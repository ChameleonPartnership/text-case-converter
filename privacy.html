Privacy Policy for Text Case Converter Pro

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Text Case Converter Pro is designed as a privacy-first, browser-based text conversion tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the site.

Client-Side Text Conversion

All text conversion happens client-side in your web browser. The text you type or paste into the converter is processed locally by JavaScript on your device. Text entered into the tool is not sent to a server by Text Case Converter Pro.

Information We Collect

Text Case Converter Pro does not require an account and does not intentionally collect the text you convert. Because the site is static, the core converter does not store your submitted text on our servers.

LocalStorage

The site uses your browser's localStorage to remember your light or dark theme preference. This value is stored on your device under the key used by the converter and is not used to identify you. You can clear it through your browser settings at any time.

Google AdSense and Cookies

Text Case Converter Pro may display advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. You can learn more from Google's advertising policies and controls at Google's Advertising Policies.

Third-Party Services

Third-party advertising services may receive information such as your browser type, device information, IP address, and ad interaction data according to their own policies. Text Case Converter Pro does not control third-party cookie practices.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies, clear localStorage, or use browser privacy controls to limit tracking. Disabling cookies should not prevent the core text conversion features from working.

Contact

For privacy questions about Text Case Converter Pro, contact the site maintainer through the project or website channel where this tool is published.
